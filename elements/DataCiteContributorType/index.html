    Enum: DataCiteContributorType

    Types of contributors to research resources from DataCite 4.6.

    These describe organizational and functional roles rather than

    specific contribution activities (see CRediT/ResearchRole for those).

    __

    URI: valuesets:DataCiteContributorType

    Permissible Values

    Value Title Meaning Description Exact Mappings Close Mappings Added Version Category Examples Note
    CONTACT_PERSON Contact Person DataCite:ContactPerson Person with knowledge of how to access, troubleshoot, or
    otherwise field issues related to the resource.
    support
    DATA_COLLECTOR Data Collector DataCite:DataCollector Person or institution responsible for finding, gathering, or
    collecting data under the guidelines of the author(s) or
    Principal Investigator (PI).
    CRediT:investigation data_work
    DATA_CURATOR Data Curator DataCite:DataCurator Person tasked with reviewing, enhancing, cleaning, or standardizing
    metadata and the associated data submitted for storage, use, and
    maintenance within a repository.
    CRediT:data-curation data_work
    DATA_MANAGER Data Manager DataCite:DataManager Person or organization responsible for maintaining the finished
    resource, including data quality, access permissions, and
    long-term availability.
    CRediT:data-curation data_work
    DISTRIBUTOR DataCite:Distributor Institution tasked with responsibility to generate or disseminate
    copies of the resource in either electronic or print form.
    marcrel:dst dissemination
    EDITOR DataCite:Editor A person who oversees the details related to the publication
    format of the resource.
    marcrel:edt CRediT:writing-review-editing editorial
    HOSTING_INSTITUTION Hosting Institution DataCite:HostingInstitution Typically, the organization allowing the resource to be available
    on the internet through the provision of its hardware, software,
    or operating support.
    infrastructure
    PRODUCER DataCite:Producer Person or organization responsible for the artistic and technical
    aspects of a resource, typically in audiovisual or media contexts.
    marcrel:pro production
    PROJECT_LEADER Project Leader DataCite:ProjectLeader Person officially designated as head of a project team or
    sub-project team instrumental in the work necessary to the
    development of the resource.
    NCIT:C19924 CRediT:project-administration leadership
    PROJECT_MANAGER Project Manager DataCite:ProjectManager Person officially designated as manager of a project, responsible
    for day-to-day management activities.
    CRediT:project-administration leadership
    PROJECT_MEMBER Project Member DataCite:ProjectMember Person on the membership list of a designated project or
    project team.
    team
    REGISTRATION_AGENCY Registration Agency DataCite:RegistrationAgency Institution or organization officially appointed by a Registration
    Authority to handle specific tasks within a defined area of
    responsibility.
    governance DataCite member organizations
    REGISTRATION_AUTHORITY Registration Authority DataCite:RegistrationAuthority A standards-setting body from which Registration Agencies obtain
    their official recognition and guidance.
    governance International DOI Foundation
    RELATED_PERSON Related Person DataCite:RelatedPerson A person without a specifically defined role in the development
    of the resource, but who is someone the author wishes to recognize.
    acknowledgment
    RESEARCHER DataCite:Researcher A person involved in analyzing data or the results of an
    experiment or formal study.
    NCIT:C25741 CRediT:formal-analysis, CRediT:investigation research
    RESEARCH_GROUP Research Group DataCite:ResearchGroup A group of individuals with a common research focus, typically
    within a lab, department, or division.
    team
    RIGHTS_HOLDER Rights Holder DataCite:RightsHolder Person or institution owning or managing property rights,
    including intellectual property rights, over the resource.
    marcrel:own, NCIT:C54131 legal
    SPONSOR DataCite:Sponsor Person or organization that issued a contract or under the
    auspices of which a work has been written, printed, published,
    developed, etc.
    marcrel:spn CRediT:funding-acquisition funding
    SUPERVISOR DataCite:Supervisor Designated administrator overseeing one or more groups or teams
    working to produce the resource.
    marcrel:ths CRediT:supervision leadership
    TRANSLATOR DataCite:Translator Person, organization, or automated system responsible for
    rendering the content of a resource from one language into
    another.
    marcrel:trl 4.6 translation
    WORK_PACKAGE_LEADER Work Package Leader DataCite:WorkPackageLeader A Work Package is a recognized data product, and the Work Package
    Leader ensures the comprehensive contents, availability, and
    quality of the work package.
    leadership
    OTHER DataCite:Other Any person or institution making a significant contribution not
    covered by other contributor type values.
    Should be accompanied by free-text description
