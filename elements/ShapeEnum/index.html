    Enum: ShapeEnum

    A morphological quality inhering in a bearer by virtue of the bearer's ratios of distances between its features. Derived from PATO shape (PATO:0000052) child values.

    URI: valuesets:ShapeEnum

    View in BioPortal

    ELLIPTIC elliptic PATO:0000947 Oval with two axes of symmetry, as produced by a conical section
    SPHEROID spheroid PATO:0001865 A convex 3-D shape obtained by rotating an ellipse about one of its principal axes
