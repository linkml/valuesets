  ZYGOTE_STAGE:
    text: ZYGOTE_STAGE
    description: Embryonic stage defined by a one-cell embryo (fertilised egg) with

    meaning: MmusDv:0000003
    title: Theiler stage 01
  TWO_CELL_STAGE:
    text: TWO_CELL_STAGE
    description: Embryonic cleavage stage defined by a dividing 2-4 cells egg. Embryonic
      age 1 dpc.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000005
    title: Theiler stage 02
  FOUR_CELL_STAGE:
    text: FOUR_CELL_STAGE
    description: Part of Theiler stage 02 - dividing egg with 4 cells. Embryonic age
      approximately 1 dpc.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000005
    title: Theiler stage 02
  EIGHT_CELL_STAGE:
    text: EIGHT_CELL_STAGE
    description: Part of early morula stage - dividing egg with 8 cells. Embryonic
      age approximately 2 dpc.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000006
    title: Theiler stage 03
  MORULA_STAGE:
    text: MORULA_STAGE
    description: Embryonic cleavage stage defined by a dividing 4-16 cells egg, early
      to fully compacted morula. Embryonic age 2 dpc.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000006
    title: Theiler stage 03
  BLASTOCYST_STAGE:
    text: BLASTOCYST_STAGE
    description: Embryonic blastula stage defined by a zona free blastocyst (zona
      pellucida absent). Embryonic age 4 dpc.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000009
    title: Theiler stage 05
  GASTRULA_STAGE:
    text: GASTRULA_STAGE
    description: Embryonic stage defined by complex and coordinated series of cellular
      movements that occurs at the end of cleavage.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000013
    title: gastrula stage
  NEURULA_STAGE:
    text: NEURULA_STAGE
    description: Embryonic stage called presomite stage and defined by the formation
      of the neural plate. This stage starts the emergence of the central nervous
      system at embryonic age 7.5 dpc.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000017
    title: Theiler stage 11
  ORGANOGENESIS_STAGE:
    text: ORGANOGENESIS_STAGE
    description: Embryonic stage at which the ectoderm, endoderm, and mesoderm develop
      into the internal organs of the organism.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000018
    title: organogenesis stage
  E0_5:
    text: E0_5
    description: Embryonic day 0.5 - one-cell embryo stage. Corresponds to Theiler
      stage 01.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000003
    title: Theiler stage 01
  E9_5:
    text: E9_5
    description: Embryonic day 9.5 - organogenesis stage with visible hind limb buds.
      Between Theiler stages 15 and 16.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000023
    title: Theiler stage 16
  E14_5:
    text: E14_5
    description: Embryonic day 14.5 - late organogenesis with clearly visible fingers
      and long bones of the limbs present.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000029
    title: Theiler stage 22
  P0_NEWBORN:
    text: P0_NEWBORN
    description: Stage that refers to the newborn mouse, aged E19-20, P0. Used for
      postnatal days 0 through 3.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000036
    title: Theiler stage 27
  P21_WEANING:
    text: P21_WEANING
    description: Weaning stage at approximately 21-22 days old. Transition from nursing
      to independent feeding.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000141
    title: 2-week-old stage
  P42_JUVENILE:
    text: P42_JUVENILE
    description: Prime adult stage at 6 weeks (42 days) old. Commonly considered the
      milestone for sexual maturity.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000151
    title: 6-week-old stage
  P56_ADULT:
    text: P56_ADULT
    description: Adult stage at 8 weeks (56 days) old. Fully mature adult mouse.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000154
    title: 8-week-old stage
  AGED_12_MONTHS:
    text: AGED_12_MONTHS
    description: Middle aged stage for mice over 10 and under 18 months old. Shows
      progressive age-related changes.
    meaning: MmusDv:0000135
    title: middle aged stage
  THEILER_STAGE:
    text: THEILER_STAGE
    description: Reference to any Theiler stage (TS1-TS28) which provides standardized
      morphological staging for mouse development.
