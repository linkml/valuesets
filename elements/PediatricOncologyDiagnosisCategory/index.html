    Enum: PediatricOncologyDiagnosisCategory

    High-level groupings for pediatric cancer diagnoses per CCDI CDE 16607972. Designed for cohort aggregation across federated data resources. Each diagnosis maps to a single dominant category.

    URI: valuesets:PediatricOncologyDiagnosisCategory

    Permissible Values

    Value Title Meaning Description Category Grade Who Classification
    ATYPICAL_TERATOID_RHABDOID_TUMOR Atypical Teratoid/Rhabdoid Tumor NCIT:C6906 Highly malignant embryonal CNS tumor characterized by loss of SMARCB1 (INI1) or SMARCA4 expression. Predominantly occurs in young children. brain_tumor WHO CNS5
    CHOROID_PLEXUS_TUMOR Choroid Plexus Neoplasm NCIT:C3473 Neoplasms arising from the choroid plexus epithelium, including papilloma, atypical papilloma, and carcinoma. brain_tumor WHO CNS5

    OTHER_GLIOMA Glioma NCIT:C3059 Glial tumors not classified as high-grade or low-grade glioma, including angiocentric glioma and astroblastoma. brain_tumor
    OTHER_BRAIN_TUMOR Brain Neoplasm NCIT:C2907 CNS tumors not fitting other brain tumor categories, including meningioma, schwannoma, and hemangioblastoma. brain_tumor
    LYMPHOBLASTIC_LEUKEMIA Lymphoblastic Leukemia NCIT:C3167 Acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL) including B-ALL and T-ALL with all molecular subtypes (BCR::ABL1, ETV6::RUNX1, KMT2A-r, DUX4, MEF2D, etc.). hematologic WHO-HAEM5
    MYELOID_LEUKEMIA Myeloid Leukemia NCIT:C3171 Acute myeloid leukemia (AML) and related myeloid neoplasms including AML with defining genetic abnormalities, therapy-related AML, and juvenile myelomonocytic leukemia (JMML). hematologic WHO-HAEM5
    HODGKIN_LYMPHOMA Hodgkin Lymphoma NCIT:C9357 Lymphoid neoplasm characterized by Reed-Sternberg cells, including classical Hodgkin lymphoma and nodular lymphocyte predominant Hodgkin lymphoma. hematologic WHO-HAEM5
    NON_HODGKIN_LYMPHOMA Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma NCIT:C3211 Lymphoid neoplasms other than Hodgkin lymphoma, including Burkitt lymphoma, diffuse large B-cell lymphoma, anaplastic large cell lymphoma, and lymphoblastic lymphoma. hematologic WHO-HAEM5
    LYMPHOPROLIFERATIVE_DISEASE Lymphoproliferative Disorder NCIT:C9308 Disorders characterized by abnormal lymphocyte proliferation, including post-transplant lymphoproliferative disorder (PTLD) and hemophagocytic lymphohistiocytosis (HLH). hematologic
    OTHER_HEME_TUMOR Hematopoietic and Lymphoid Cell Neoplasm NCIT:C27134 Hematologic malignancies not classified elsewhere, including histiocytic disorders, myelodysplastic syndromes, and myeloproliferative neoplasms. hematologic
    NEUROBLASTOMA Neuroblastoma NCIT:C3270 Embryonal tumor of the peripheral sympathetic nervous system, arising from neural crest cells. Includes ganglioneuroblastoma and ganglioneuroma. solid_tumor WHO Pediatric Blue Book
    OSTEOSARCOMA Osteosarcoma NCIT:C9145 Primary malignant bone tumor characterized by osteoid production, including conventional, telangiectatic, and small cell variants. solid_tumor WHO Bone/Soft Tissue
    EWING_SARCOMA Ewing Sarcoma NCIT:C4817 Small round cell sarcoma typically harboring EWSR1 rearrangements, arising in bone or soft tissue. Includes Ewing sarcoma family of tumors. solid_tumor WHO Bone/Soft Tissue
    RHABDOMYOSARCOMA Rhabdomyosarcoma NCIT:C3359 Malignant soft tissue tumor with skeletal muscle differentiation, including embryonal, alveolar, and spindle cell/sclerosing subtypes. solid_tumor WHO Bone/Soft Tissue
    SOFT_TISSUE_TUMOR Soft Tissue Sarcoma NCIT:C9306 Soft tissue neoplasms other than rhabdomyosarcoma and Ewing sarcoma, including synovial sarcoma, fibrosarcoma, and other sarcomas. Also includes non-sarcomatous soft tissue tumors. solid_tumor
    RHABDOID_TUMOR Rhabdoid Tumor NCIT:C3808 Highly aggressive tumors characterized by SMARCB1 loss, occurring outside the CNS (extracranial rhabdoid tumor). Includes malignant rhabdoid tumor of kidney. solid_tumor
    RENAL_TUMOR Malignant Kidney Neoplasm NCIT:C7548 Kidney tumors including Wilms tumor (nephroblastoma), clear cell sarcoma of kidney, renal cell carcinoma, and congenital mesoblastic nephroma. solid_tumor WHO Pediatric Blue Book
    RETINOBLASTOMA Retinoblastoma NCIT:C7541 Malignant neoplasm of the retina arising from developing retinal cells, associated with RB1 mutations. solid_tumor WHO Pediatric Blue Book
    GERM_CELL_TUMOR Germ Cell Tumors NCIT:C3708 Tumors arising from primordial germ cells, occurring in gonadal or extragonadal sites. Includes teratoma, yolk sac tumor, germinoma, choriocarcinoma, and mixed germ cell tumors. Excludes CNS germ cell tumors. solid_tumor
    ENDOCRINE_AND_NEUROENDOCRINE_TUMOR Endocrine Neoplasm NCIT:C3010 Tumors of endocrine glands and neuroendocrine cells, including thyroid carcinoma, adrenocortical carcinoma, pheochromocytoma, and paraganglioma. solid_tumor
    OTHER_SOLID_TUMOR Childhood Solid Neoplasm NCIT:C9107 Solid tumors not classified elsewhere, including hepatoblastoma, pleuropulmonary blastoma, nasopharyngeal carcinoma, melanoma, and carcinomas. solid_tumor
